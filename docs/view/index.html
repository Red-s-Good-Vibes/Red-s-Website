What is Red's Good Vibes?

We are a sibling run 501c3 approved non-profit corporation called Red's Good Vibes Inc. We are a mobile giving organization started in honor of our brother “Red”. Whatever our community is dealing with, whatever challenges they have, we want to be there to support them. It is our mission to provide support and relief to our local communities with a smile and no judgement. Currently we are providing groceries to families and people in the community affected by the COVID-19 closures. We do no contact deliveries and only require an address, family size and ages of any children so that we can discreetly deliver to those in need and reduce the hesitation in reaching out for help. We are attempting to fill the gaps that exist with the food insecure members of our community being able to access a consistent, safe food source. Unlike food pantries, or school programs we are not limiting the people we serve to once a month, or to having to arrange to pick up their food items. We mobilize quickly and can immediately address the need by bringing the assistance to them.
